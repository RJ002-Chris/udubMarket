ABOUT US

Mission Statement

  At the end of the school year, college students often find themselves moving out of their apartments and dorms. During this time, students try to pack all there stuff to bring back or move to their new place, but oftenly, students will look to get rid of their old belongings or try to sell them. These students go out and search for people they can off load their stuff to, such as friends, class-mates, underclassmen moving in, etc. This website looks to help provide students with an online markertplace where students can effectively find buyers and sellers for their belongings, a place where there is consistency in the product details and easier to browse and organize the items by type of product. This will greatly help students moving in or out of their apartments and dorms and make the process faster and easier. We aim to relieve students of any more stress while giving them more time to do other things such as studying or project work.

Website Features

  The current features for this website include a naviagation bar at the top of the screen, a search bar where students can search using keywords to look for the items they want, a list of cards in the middle of the page where users can browse and look at the various items listed on the online marketplace, and a form at the body with contact information incase users might want to report something or contact us.
